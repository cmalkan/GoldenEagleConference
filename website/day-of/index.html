Your Arrival Flow

Three steps from parking lot to your seat — takes about 10 minutes.

01

Step 01

Check In

Head to the Registration Table in the lobby — this is your one-stop shop:

  • Show your ID to confirm your registration.
  • Pick up your exclusive NFC-enabled MOCS 2026 challenge coin.
  • Grab your printed agenda for the day.

02

Step 02

Box lunch — free for all registered attendees. Pick it up 11:00 – 11:30 AM at the registration table.

03

Step 03

Get Settled

Head to AMU Room 227 — the main hall. Find a seat, enjoy your lunch, and network before the keynote kicks off.

How to Find Us on Campus

Short walkthrough from common arrival points to the AMU building. Save this page on your phone before you leave.

Once inside

AMU 2nd floor — rooms are all next to each other

Head up to the 2nd floor. Rooms 227, 252, and 254 sit right next to each other. Look for the MOCS wayfinding sign in the hallway — it'll point you to the right room for your session.
