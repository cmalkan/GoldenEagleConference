Call for Presenters & Labs

We are actively accepting talk and hands-on lab proposals for MOCS. Submission deadline: April 1st. We welcome practitioner-led sessions that deliver actionable takeaways for defenders and builders.

What we are looking for

  • OT/ICS threat detection, defense strategy, and incident response
  • Critical infrastructure security and resilience engineering
  • Threat hunting, forensics, and attack simulation (red/blue)
  • Secure architecture, SDLC, risk governance, and compliance
  • Hands-on labs with practical workflows and measurable outcomes

Presenter details

  • Audience levels: beginner, intermediate, and advanced
  • Session formats: 30, 45, 60, and 90-minute options
  • Notification timeline: accepted speakers notified after review window closes
  • Lab proposals should include environment/setup requirements
