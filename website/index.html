Why this summit matters

This summit is structured as an inclusive, career-spanning security experience—not just a one-day event. We connect students, career changers, practitioners, and employers through OT and critical infrastructure content, hands-on labs, and mentorship opportunities.

🛡️ OT & Critical Infrastructure First

Focused on OT systems, utility environments, and critical infrastructure segments where operational impact is highest.

🔧 Hands-On by Design

Lab-first sessions and practical exercises centered on real-world defense patterns and incident windows.

🎓 Career Pathways Across Levels

Direct links between employers, students, early-career talent, and mentors to support coaching, networking, and long-term career growth.
