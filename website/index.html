Why this first summit matters

This inaugural event is built to seed an ongoing professional security network—not just a one-day conference. We connect OT and critical infrastructure security practitioners with university talent and long-term community mentorship.

🛡️ OT & Critical Infrastructure First

Focused on OT systems, utility environments, and critical infrastructure segments where operational impact is highest.

🔧 Hands-On by Design

Lab-first sessions and practical exercises centered on real-world defense patterns and incident windows.

🤝 University + Community Pipeline

Direct links between employers, students, and mentors to support coaching, networking, and long-term career growth.
