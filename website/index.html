Call for Speakers — MOCS 2027

We're picking ~12 sessions across 3 rooms — submit yours by March 5, 2027.

What we're looking for

Talks across OT cybersecurity

MOCS is a community-driven OT cybersecurity summit. We're looking for talks from anyone defending, building, or breaking operational-technology systems — practitioners, plant engineers, SOC teams, students, and educators all welcome.

  • Topics — ICS / OT defense, threat detection, IR, GRC & standards (IEC 62443), pen testing, supply chain, network segmentation, hands-on labs, career & community
  • Formats — 30-min talk (25 + 5 Q&A) · panel (45 min) · hands-on / demo (45 min)
  • We love — war stories, lessons-learned, deep technical, first-time speakers, students presenting capstone work
