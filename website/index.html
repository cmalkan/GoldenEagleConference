Why Attend MOCS?

A practical, community-driven OT cybersecurity conference for defenders, students, and hiring teams. OT means Operational Technology, including manufacturing systems, industrial controls, and ICS environments across many sectors.

🎓 Career Growth

Resume workshops, mentor networking, and hiring-focused sessions.

🛡️ OT Hands-On Learning

Real-world OT and ICS topics across manufacturing, industrial control, and infrastructure environments.

✅ CPE Credits

Earn Continuing Professional Education credits through attendance.
